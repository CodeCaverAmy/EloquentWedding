Services

From your complimentary consultation to the recording of your signed marriage license, I will be with you every step of the way.

Planning

Creating a Customized Ceremony

The three of us will meet so we can get to know each other. You’ll share your ideas and vision for your wedding. I’ll provide a diverse selection of vows, readings and ideas. Or, you can provide your own. You’ll have a completely customized ceremony just for you. We’ll communicate by email or phone during the planning process.

After the consult, you’ll have all the information you need and have time to decide if I’m the right officiant for you.

Rehearsal

Practice for the Big Day

We’ll practice all the parts of the ceremony so everyone knows where to be and what to do.

Voila! You and everyone in your wedding party will feel confident about the flow of the ceremony so that you can relax and fully enjoy your big day.

Ceremony

Eloquently Deliver Your Memorable Ceremony

Your big day will be coordinated to make sure everyone’s in the right places at the right times. You won't need to stress over the logistics; I'll make sure everything goes smoothly.

Your unique ceremony will be masterfully conducted with confidence and enthusiasm.

Afterward, I’ll submit your signed marriage license to the county Register of Deeds to have it recorded.

About Me

Officiating weddings has become my passion and I look forward to helping you on your special day. I’m detail oriented, creative and experienced, having officiated weddings since 2013. I will listen to you, research, communicate and collaborate with you to ensure your wedding ceremony is amazing.

I provide my services within a sixty mile radius of Milwaukee.
Fees vary depending on whichever of my services you'd like for your wedding.
